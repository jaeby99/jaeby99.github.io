PAICLAB aims to conduct research on privacy-preserving machine learning (PPML) with cryptographic tools.
We are working mainly on primitives in fully homomorphic encryption (FHE), advanced algorithms of FHE, and efficient PPML with deep neural networks.
 Our interest also includes hardware optimization for PPML on FHE and practical implementation of PPML for AI-as-a-service (Alaas) cloud systems.

We are looking for students interested in researching private AI and cryptography, inlcuding the latest cryptographic techniques such as homomorphic encryption, post-quantum cryptography, and multi-party computation.
